Continuously asked questions

What types of juices do you offer?

We offer a diverse range of juices, including refreshing citrus blends, Milkshakes, fresh juice, Jigarthanda, tropical fruit medleys, and healthy green concoctions. Explore our menu to discover the perfect juice to suit your taste.

Can I customize my milkshake?

Absolutely! Our milkshakes are customizable to your liking. Choose your base flavor, add extra toppings, and create a personalized milkshake that caters to your cravings.

Do you offer dairy-free ice cream options

Yes, we understand dietary preferences! Our menu includes dairy-free ice cream options made with plant-based ingredients, ensuring everyone can indulge in a delightful frozen treat.

What is the specialty of your Jigarthanda?

Our Jigarthanda is a special blend of traditional flavors, featuring creamy textures, falooda noodles, and a touch of aromatic ingredients. It's a must-try for those seeking an authentic and delightful experience.

Is there an option for online food delivery?

Absolutely! We offer convenient online food delivery services. Simply browse our menu, place your order online, and savor the goodness of our treats delivered right to your doorstep.

Do you have any loyalty programs for regular customers?

Yes, we value our loyal customers! Consider joining our loyalty program for exclusive perks, discounts, and special offers. It's our way of saying thank you for choosing us for your delightful refreshments.
